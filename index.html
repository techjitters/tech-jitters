Unravel With Data Analysis

Rules for the competition are:

1. Students have to participate in a team of two.

2. Entry without registration and respective college ID cards will not be allowed in any case.

3. Participants must bring their own laptops with preinstalled language or framework of their choice in the event.

Participants can use any programming language of his/her choice (Python, R, SQL, Java, etc.)

4.The participants will be given a data set of information and a problem statement related to it.

5. After analysing the problem statement, participants are required to provide a desired solution.

6. Participants are not allowed to take any help from any online sources. Doing this will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

7. Usage of mobile phones is strictly prohibited during competition.

8. Time Duration for the competition is 2 hours. No extra time will be provided.

9. In case of any dispute, Judge’s decision will be final.

10. Three winners (First Position, Runner-Up and Second Runner-Up) will be awarded at the end of the event with cash prizes and certificates.

IT Quiz

Rules for the competition are:

1. Students have to participate in a team of two.

2. Entry without registration and respective college ID cards will not be allowed in any case.

3. Participants are not allowed to take any help from any online sources. Doing this will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

4. Questions will be related to general IT awareness.

5.In case of any dispute, Judge’s decision will be final.

6. Three winners (First Position, Runner-Up and Second Runner-Up) will be awarded at the end of the event with cash prizes and certificates.


Three rounds:

Round I - screening round. 25 Questions (MCQs only) with a time limit of 30 minutes. All the participating teams eligible.
Top 12 teams will proceed to ROUND II.

Round II - Teams will be divided in two groups of 6 teams each. Every team will be asked three direct questions.
Top three teams from each group will proceed to ROUND III.
